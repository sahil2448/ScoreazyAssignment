Frequently Asked Questions

How much time does this course require daily?

+

The course is designed to fit into busy family schedules. Each day requires just 15-20 minutes of focused activity time with your child, plus optional extended activities for weekends.

What if my child is shy or resistant to activities?

+

Our program is specifically designed for shy children! We start with very gentle, low-pressure activities and gradually build up. Many of our most successful students started as the shyest ones.

Do I need any special materials or tools?

+

No special materials needed! All activities use common household items like paper, crayons, and everyday objects. We provide a complete list of materials at the start of the course.

What if we miss a few days? Can we catch up?

+

The course is self-paced and you have lifetime access. You can pause, restart, or repeat any week as needed. We also provide catch-up guides for busy weeks.

Is there any support if we have questions?

+

Yes! You get access to our private parent community and weekly Q&A sessions with our child psychology experts. Plus, email support throughout your journey.
